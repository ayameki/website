Tournament Organizer

Aegis Esports

Aegis Esports is a North American Esports organization that was founded in May of 2021. It strives to create a path to professional esports, and bring a player first approach to its community. Apart from providing high quality amateur leagues, they also take pride in the services they provide. From their engaging community, to their high quality streams, to their extensive in-depth stat resources, Aegis Esports emphasizes quality above all.

As the tournament organizer, Nancy is the lead of the Platinum capped divison. Her responsibilities include but not limited to clarifying and enforcing the rules laid out in the rulebook, maintaining consistent leadership and order to ensure the tournament runs smoothly, recording tournament results and making those results available afterwards for participants.
